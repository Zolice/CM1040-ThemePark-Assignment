About

Our Vision: Co-Existence between Wildlife and People

Our Mission: We rescue endangered species, spread awareness about extinction of wildlife, most importantly, provide fun, interactive and inspirational experience for everyone at our park.


The (insert park name here) is home to around 2,000 animals from over 300 different species comprising of Land and Marine Wildlife. Different parts of the park are designed to mimic different habitats to cater for the different wildlife.

We are committed to the conservation of wildlife on our planet by providing an engaging experience to our visitors. Not only is the park suitable for sightseeing, but we also offer other unique activities like Interacting with Wildlife, Presentations, Safari Tours. (If got other unique activity pls add in here also thanks)

“For a child – or even an adult – being in the presence of a living, breathing animal, knowing the way it smells, looking into his or her eyes, and above all, sensing its “being-ness” can be a life-changing experience.” – Jane Goodall, Primatologist.

We believe that education is a key aspect of wildlife conservation and environmental protection. The lack of knowledge about the importance of conserving biodiversity for the wellbeing and health of wildlife will cause deterioration of ecosystems, creating negative consequences in the medium- and long-term sustainability of the population. Hence, we also offer educational programs that are comprehensive, robust and immersive for children and youths to enjoy.
