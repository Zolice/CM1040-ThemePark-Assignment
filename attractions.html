Aquarium

 Over 120,000 marine animals representing 1,300 species that inhabit over 60 habitats, complemented by close-up animal encounters, and excursions that can hopefully inspire visitors to protect the oceans around the globe.

Rides

 An unorthodox attraction in a wildlife park, we offer different rides. Ranging from a slow, soothing tall ride in the park, gaining a bird's-eye view of the park and enjoy a different perspective than on ground, to getting a thrilling experience in our roller coaster where you enjoy the spin, flips and fast rides.

Exotic Animals

 Catch a glimpse of exotic animals in different areas of our park. Exotic animals include but not limited to:
Pandas, the distinctive black and white animal is a national treasure in China.
Black Rhinos, the smaller of the two African rhino species is native to eastern and southern Africa.
