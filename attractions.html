Roller Coasters

An unorthodox attraction in a wildlife park, we offer different rides. Ranging from a slow, soothing tall ride in the park, gaining a bird's-eye view of the park and enjoy a different perspective than on ground, to getting a thrilling experience in our roller coaster where you enjoy the spin, flips and fast rides.
