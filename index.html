Visitor Reviews

Best Zoo I've been to!

"This zoo has 4 different zones, each with it's own habitat and their respective animals. My family went to the zoo together for the entire sunday, and we only managed to visit 3 out of 4 of the zones!

The zoo is very well maintained, and the animals are well taken care of. The zoo is also very spacious, and there are plenty of benches and shaded areas for visitors to rest. The zoo is also very clean, and the staff are very friendly and helpful."
-Jacob Leo

Great for kids!

"Loved the Roller Coaster rides! Didn’t expect one in a zoo! My kid enjoyed seeing the animals from different parts of the world. She was particularly fond of the Pandas!"
-Zack Yong

Beautiful Aquarium

"I love aquariums, and this is easily one of the best ones in the world. Such a varied display of fish and marine life, and highly informative. The final tank measuring 45m with 70cm glass in an engineering marvel and full of amazing fish including sharks, manta rays and huge grouper. Great for kids of all ages, and even adults!"
-Jia Sheng

Relaxing Scenery

"It was a wonderful experience and there is vast range of animals to look at. I took the safari tour, and the speed of the tram wasn't too fast, it gave us ample time to have a good view at every location along the way. This is a great place to get surrounded by the lush, green plants and all kinds of animals."
-Hui Toon
